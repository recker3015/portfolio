    Frequently Asked Questions

    If you don't see what's on your mind, reach out to us anytime.

    What is your refund policy?

    If you are not happy with our service, Portfoliobox offers 14 days money back guarantee.

    What forms of payment do you accept?

    We accept credit cards (Visa, Master & Maestro).

    Can I create an online store using portfoliobox?

    Yes, you can easily set up your online store using Portfoliobox and the good thing is - we do not charge any commission fee!

    Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription plan?

    Of course! You can upgrade your subscription plan at any time directly in your admin panel. You can also downgrade your subscription from Pro Plus to Pro. Get in touch with our support team if you have any questions regarding your subscription.

    Can I switch my subscription from monthly to yearly and vice-versa?

    Yes. We can switch your subscription from monthly to yearly and from yearly to monthly. Please contact our support team and we’ll convert your subscription.

    Does the PRO accounts come with a domain name?

    Yes, we can register a custom domain name for you if you choose the Pro or Pro Plus account. You can also use your own domain name.

    Can I change my custom domain name in the middle of my subscription?

    Yes, if you would like to change your custom domain name, please contact our support team.

    Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

    Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your admin panel.

    How can I contact your support team?

    Our support team can be reached via email and live chat. The Email address is support@portfoliobox.net.
